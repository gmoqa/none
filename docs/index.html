Why NONE exists

For None, our son

None is my son with severe autism, and he is nonverbal. When we needed a better way to communicate, we looked for apps but they weren't as simple and direct as we needed. So I built NONE.

It doesn't have complicated features or subscriptions. Just the basics: buttons, images, sounds. We keep improving it week by week.

If it helps other families, then this publication was worth it.
